The Story Behind the Coach

BAD HABITS
ALMOST KILLED ME.

```

From 13 to 23, I was a full-blown addict. Drugs. Alcohol. A decade of bad decisions that nearly cost me everything. I wasn't just struggling — I was lost. And I had no idea how to find my way back.

Getting sober didn't hand me a perfect life. It handed me something better: a system. Faith. Muay Thai. Nutrition. Structure. A plan I followed like my life depended on it — because it did. That system became the foundation of everything I've built since.

The Meatball Shop. Seamore's. Kreatures of Habit. Competing as an athlete. Becoming the husband and father I wanted to be. Not one day of that happened without the habits I protect every single morning.

"Bad habits almost killed me — and great habits have made me the man I am today. That's why I coach."

I built Fitter Fathers because I know what it takes to change — and I know most men just need the right system and someone in their corner. That's what I'm here for.
